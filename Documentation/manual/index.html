Welcome to MQMyUI

MQMyUI is a complete custom interface for your character. It replaces a pile of separate UI add-ons with one tidy package: floating bars for your health, mana, and endurance; buff and song timers; a spell gem bar; a casting bar; group and raid windows; an inventory and bag browser; an Alternate Advancement (AA) manager; and more. Every window can be shown, hidden, moved, resized, scaled, recolored, and themed to taste.

If you play several characters at once, MQMyUI shines: your boxes share their health, buffs, AA, and items with each other automatically, so one window can show your whole team and let you act on any of them.

Quick Start

  1. Load the interface by typing /plugin MQMyUI in the game.
  2. A small Toolbar of buttons appears. This is your launcher — every other window starts hidden.
  3. Click a Toolbar button (or type /myui <name>) to open a window. Click the Settings button to start customizing.
Nothing is locked in. You can show or hide any window at any time, and your layout and styling are saved per character — so each of your characters can look completely different.
